AI Notebook on Telegram

Ask questions, save a personal Q&A library, and get AI answers — all from your favourite chat app. The bot shares the same AI engine as this web app.

Bot commands

  • /start — introduction & quick help
  • /help — list all commands
  • /add — save a question & answer
  • /list — view your saved Q&A
  • /delete — remove a saved item
  • Send any message — get an instant AI answer

Getting started

  • Open Telegram and search for the AI Notebook bot.
  • Tap Start to begin a conversation.
  • Send /help to see everything it can do.
  • Your bot library and web account stay in sync.
