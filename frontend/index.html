Our AI is powered by Groq's llm, one of the most advanced language models available. It's trained on vast amounts of educational content and provides highly accurate, well-structured answers.
Can I use it on mobile?
Absolutely! Study Sphere AI is fully optimized for mobile devices. You can study on the go with the same powerful features available on desktop.
Is my data private?
Yes, your data is completely private and secure. We use industry-standard encryption and never share your information with third parties.
Can I export my notes?
Yes! You can export your notes, quizzes, and flashcards in multiple formats including PDF, Markdown, and more.
